The world has made a paramount shift from being real (offline) to being online. But the upheaval in silicon valley dictates, tech advances will not stop here. We are moving towards a world not just being heavily dependent on the internet but being IN the internet. This idea which seems science fiction rightly originated in a science fiction book called Snow Crash, however, does not seem fiction anymore. What is METAVERSE ?

We already have small bytes of metaverses in the form of games such as Fortnite and Roblox. Google with its Google glass has made substantial progress with its augmented reality tech. But metaverse will be one step ahead of all this. It will not be a virtual space where one can log in and hang out, it will be THE place we all will live in. Instead of going on social media platforms, we will have an avatar that can directly socialize and meet people in real-time through virtual reality. Emails and office meetings will be carried out in the same way. Maybe a bunch of friends can get together and watch a two-dimensional movie in a three-dimensional virtual world. Metaverse is EMBODIED INTERNET. You can imagine metaverse where you can walk into any experience or activity, and potentially address all your needs from a single starting point or world that is also populated with everyone else you know. While VR is all about a world created solely inside computers or online, AR still deals with the real world and has elements of the virtual world built atop it, akin to layers of information.
